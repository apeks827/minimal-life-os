# Product

LifeInbox helps people put unstructured life noise into one calm place. The core loop is: capture anything, let AI classify it, review the created object, then see it reflected in the day plan and life balance.

## MVP Scope

- RU-first interface with EN switch.
- Auth, onboarding, and user settings.
- Inbox capture with AI or local fallback classification.
- Today Plan, Tasks, Goals, Habits, Calendar, Balance Wheel, Suggestions, Settings.
- Safety posture: supportive, non-medical, non-legal, non-financial, no shame, no manipulation.

## Out Of Scope For MVP

- Real push notifications beyond schema/config placeholders.
- Full offline sync.
- Production billing and team collaboration.
